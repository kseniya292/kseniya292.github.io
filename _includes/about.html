About

In my previous life I worked in accounting and finance, but my real passion lies in designing and building beautiful websites. I am a problem solver by nature and enjoy the technical side of development, but I also have a creative side that I love to use in web design. I enjoy working directly with my clients and helping small businesses and non-profits improve their digital presence.

My skills : HTML | CSS | Javascript | jQuery | Wordpress | Node.js | Express.js | MongoDB | AngularJS | Photoshop | Illustrator

I am a co-founder of Women in Coding, a local group that provides resources and opportunities to those interested in learning to code in our community.
